Special Operations Group

The Special Operations Group (SOG) is part of the Security Forces of the US Air Force and is primarily tasked with providing specialized security and force protection missions. These airmen are trained to protect air bases, aircraft, and personnel in high-threat environments. They are a highly skilled unit with advanced training in counterterrorism, direct action, and airfield security in hostile or denied areas.
